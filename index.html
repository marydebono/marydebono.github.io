About Me

I’m Dr. Mary Debono, DDS, and I’m passionate about providing personalized, comprehensive dental care for patients of all ages. I completed both my undergraduate degree (2018) and Doctor of Dental Surgery (2022) at the University of Toronto, followed by a 1 year general practice residency at Carolinas Medical Center in North Carolina.


I have a special focus on assessing and treating infant tongue and lip ties, helping families support their babies’ feeding, comfort, and early oral development. I take a multidisciplinary approach to tongue tie therapy and review treatment options including, but not limited to, tongue tie release surgery.


I take an individualized approach to understand your goals and to educate and empower you to make treatment decisions that best suit your family. I aim to make the experience reassuring and collaborative - helping families understand the process, address any concerns, and set their little ones up for healthy growth from the very beginning.
